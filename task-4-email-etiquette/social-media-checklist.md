# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments  
- Think carefully before posting or sharing content  
- Verify information before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation  
- Use privacy settings to control who can view your content  
- Showcase your achievements, skills, and positive activities  

## Don’ts
- Do not share sensitive personal information such as phone number, address, or passwords  
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Do not engage in arguments, trolling, or negative interactions  
- Avoid sharing unverified news or fake information  
- Do not overshare private details that may harm your reputation
