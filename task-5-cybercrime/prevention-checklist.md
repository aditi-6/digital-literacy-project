## Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown UPI collect requests  
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts  
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Regularly check bank statements and transaction history  
- Do not install apps from unknown or untrusted sources  

### UPI Safety Tips
- Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN  
- Double-check payment requests before approving  

### Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
